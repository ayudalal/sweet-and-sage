about the blog

Welcome to Sweet and Sage, a space we created to step away from day-to-day stress and worry. We share easy recipes for desserts, treats, and drinks like coffee and matcha. Everything here is meant to be simple, approachable, and stress-free, no complicated steps or fancy tools needed. Our hope is that these recipes bring a little comfort into your day. Whether you're baking something just for yourself or making a snack to share with friends or family, we want this blog to be a place you can always come back to for something calming and sweet.
